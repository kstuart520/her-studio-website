About Me

Every woman deserves to be seen.

Hi, I'm Kim — a photographer based in Luling, TX with a passion for capturing real, honest, beautiful moments. Whether I'm photographing a brand-new family, a growing belly, or helping a woman see herself in a whole new light, I show up with care and intention every single time.

I believe the best photographs don't feel posed. They feel like a memory — something you can hold onto and come back to years from now.

Outside of Her Studio, I run Unveil Her Studio — a private boudoir experience for women who are ready to celebrate themselves. It's intimate, empowering, and completely on your terms.
